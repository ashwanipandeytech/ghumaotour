Program ( 6 days )

  • Day 1

    Delhi – Vrindavan –Mathura -Agra

    Morning drive to Agra ( 235 Kms / 5 hrs) en-route visit Vrindavan “ the famous Bankey Bihari Temple , Prem Mandir and then proceed to Mathura, visit Krishna Janmabhumi and then proceed to Agra . On arrival transfer to Hotel. Overnight stay at Agra.

  • Day 2

    Agra

    After breakfast , proceed for sightseeing of Agra visiting Taj Mahal, one of the 7th wonders of the world, Sikandara Fort and Agra Fort. Evening visit local Market .Overnight stay at Agra. (Taj Mahal closed on every Friday). .

  • Day 3

    Agra - Fatehpur Sikri - Ranthambore

    Morning drive to Ranthambore (240 kms / 4 hrs. ). Enroute visit Fatehpur Sikri. Ranthambore National Park, one of the most eminent wild life parks. It is consider as one of the best places in the country to see the Bengal Tiger.On arrival transfer to Hotel. Overnight stay at Ranthambore.

  • Day 4

    Ranthambhore - Jaipur

    Morning visit safari at Ranthambhore (07:00 hrs.. - 10:00 hrs..). Later drive to Jaipur ( 190 kms / 4 hrs. drive). On arrival transfer to Hotel. Evening free for shopping. Overnight stay.

  • Day 5

    Jaipur

    Morning after breakfast , proceed to Amer Fort and here you can enjoy Elephant / Jeep ride . Later proceed for Jaipur sightseeing , visit City Palace, Albert Hall , Jantar Mantar - the Royal Observatory and Hawa Mahal (drive through). Overnight stay at Jaipur.

  • Day 6

    Jaipur - Delhi

    Morning drive back to Delhi (265 kms / 5 hrs. ). Tour Concludes.


Note : Ranthambore wild life Sanctuary will remain closed from July to September.
