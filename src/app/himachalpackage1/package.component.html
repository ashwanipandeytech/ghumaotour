Program ( 6 days )

  • Day 1

    Delhi - Shimla (380 kms . / 10 hrs.).

    Morning departure to Shimla. Reach Shimla in the evening and check in to the hotel. Evening enjoy the Mall road of Shimla and the Ridge. Overnight Stay.

  • Day 2

    Shimla - Kufri - Shimla

    After breakfast, proceed to visit Kufri the famous tourist destination known for snow-laden peaks, skiing and Zoo. Also visit Christ Church in Kufri . Evening visit the beautiful Mall Road of Shimla . Overnight Stay at Shimla.

  • Day 3

    Shimla - Manali (260 kms . / 9 hrs.)

    After breakfast proceed to Manali ,pass through Beas river, Sutlej River and the beautiful Kullu Valley. (surrounded by Shivalik Ranges of Himalayas is famous for Apple Trees). Reach Manali in the evening. Evening visit local market. Overnight Stay at Manali.

  • Day 4

    Manali - Rohtang Pass - Gulaba - Manali

    Enjoy spectacular drive towards the famous point of Manali “Rohtang Snow Point” at an altitude of 3,980 mts. Here you can snow activities here such as skiing, snow scooter ride, and horse riding to make the most of your Manali Tour (Rohtang Pass is closed on Tuesday). While returning also visit Solang valley –the famous point for skiing in winter and in Summer famous for parachuting, zorbing, paragliding, and horse riding.Evening return to Manali. Overnight Stay at Manali.

  • Day 5

    Manali - Chandigarh (285 kms . / 9 hrs.).

    Morning proceed to Chandigarh .Arrive Chandigarh in the evening. En-route visit Pinjore Garden, Rock Garden, Rose Garden and boat ride in Sukhana Lake. Overnight Stay at Chandigarh.

  • Day 6

    Chandigarh - Delhi (265 kms . / 5 hrs..).

    Proceed to Delhi ,enroute visit Kurukshetra (if time permits) and then drop to Airport /Railway station for your onward journey. Tour Concludes with nice memories.

Note: As per National Green Tribunal, only local Himachal taxis are allowed to Rohtang pass. Charges are extra.
